Musical Time Machine

What's this all about?

Music can take you back to a particular time in your life. By listening to the same songs that were hits during a period of time, you can be transported back and relieve a moment in your life. While this app can't physically take you back in time, it can go back and find the music that was in the top 100 charts and was being played all over the radio so that you can relieve that period of time once more.
